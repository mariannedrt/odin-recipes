Lasagna Recipe

This is a recipe to bake a good lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna layers
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Olive Oil

Instructions:

  1. Switch on the oven
  2. Pick a dish, put olive oil in it.
  3. Put a layer of lasagna, then a layer of bolognese sauce, then a layer of bechamel sauce, then sprinkle some grated cheese. Repeat until you run out of bolognese sauce.
  4. The last layer should be lasagna, then bechamel sauce, then grated cheese.
  5. Put the dish in the oven and bake for 45 minutes.

Enjoy your lasagna!
