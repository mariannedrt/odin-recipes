Ratatouille

Despite what told Pixar, we don't use the "rat" in "ratatouille" for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggplants
  • 4 Zuchini
  • 2 onions
  • 6 Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Provence Herbs
  • Bay Leaves

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Cut the eggplants, zuchinis, onions and tomatoes in thin slices.
  3. In a pot, put some olive oil, then add the onions and garlic, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the tomatoes, salt, herbs and bay leaves, cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Pinch the veggetables with a fork to check if they are cooked, let them cook for a few more minutes if they are not
  6. et voila! It just a boiled veggetables side die, tbh. Best served with a nice piece of meat.

Enjoy your ratatouille!
