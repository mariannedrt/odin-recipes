Fried Rice

Delicious and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Rice leftover
  • Eggs
  • Ham
  • Frozen peas
  • Oyster sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Sunflower oil
  • Sesame oil
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet, scramble the eggs.
  2. Drop the rice on your scrambled eggs, break it down and add your condiments.
  3. Add ham and peas.
  4. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Taste and fix the taste with more condiments if needed.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your fried rice!
